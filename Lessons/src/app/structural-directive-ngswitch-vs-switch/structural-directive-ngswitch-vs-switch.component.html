Example of *ngSwitch vs @switch()

*ngSwitch
Congratulations on your outstanding achievement - First Grade
Congratulations on your achievement - Second Grade
Congratulations on your achievement - Third Grade
No result found



@switch()
@switch (grade) { @case(1){ Congratulations on your outstanding achievement - First Grade } @case(2){ Congratulations on your achievement - Second Grade } @case(3){ Congratulations on your achievement - Third Grade } @default { No result found } }
